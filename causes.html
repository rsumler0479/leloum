Primary School Desks

Imagine a classroom where eager young minds gather to learn, explore, and grow. Now, envision a place where access to quality education is a challenge, and even a simple desk can be a rarity. We invite you to be a part of our "Empower Learning" campaign, where each desk you provide becomes a stepping stone toward brighter futures for the children in Lelou.

🪑 A Desk, A World of Possibilities In this village, education is a beacon of hope, but the absence of basic infrastructure impedes the journey of its children. A desk is more than just a piece of furniture; it's a gateway to knowledge, a place to dream, and a symbol of commitment to the power of education.

Hospital Cafeteria 🥗

Feeding Bodies, Nourishing Souls In this humble village, access to proper nutrition is a constant struggle. The hospital cafeteria isn't just a place to eat; it's a source of strength, a beacon of comfort, and a hub of community connection. Unfortunately, the cafeteria faces challenges in providing nutritious meals due to limited resources. This is where your support becomes a lifeline.

Join us in making a real difference in the lives of the wonderful people in Lelou through our "Nourish the Village" fundraising effort. Every meal, every contribution, and every act of kindness can bring hope, health, and happiness to those who need it most.
